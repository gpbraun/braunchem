a. Magnésio
b. Alumínio
c. Bismuto
d. Arsênio
e. Cobre

$$Mg:\;[Ne]\,3s^{2}$$
$$Al:\;[Ne]\,3s^2\,3p^{1}$$
$$Bi:\;[Xe]\,6s^{2}\,4f^{14}\,5d^{10}\,6p^{3}$$
$$As:\;[Kr]\,4s^{2}\,3d^{10}\,4p^{3}$$
$$Cu:\;[Ar]\,4s^{1}\,3d^{10}$$

$$\underset{0}{\color{red}\boxed{\uparrow\downarrow}}$$

$$n=3\;;\;l=1\;;\;m_{l}=-1$$

$$n=6\;;\;l=1\;;\;m_{l}=1$$
